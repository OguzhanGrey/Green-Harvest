Nutrition Guide

Learn about the nutritional benefits of our fresh vegetables

Why Vegetables Matter

Vegetables are an essential part of a healthy diet, providing vital nutrients, fiber, and antioxidants that help maintain good health and prevent various diseases.

  • Rich in vitamins and minerals
  • High in dietary fiber
  • Low in calories
  • Contains antioxidants

Nutritional Information

Potatoes

Calories 77 kcal
Carbohydrates 17g
Protein 2g
Fiber 2.2g

Rich in potassium, vitamin C, and B vitamins. Good source of energy.

Carrots

Calories 41 kcal
Carbohydrates 10g
Protein 0.9g
Fiber 2.8g

High in beta carotene, fiber, and antioxidants. Promotes eye health.

Broccoli

Calories 55 kcal
Carbohydrates 11g
Protein 3.7g
Fiber 2.6g

Contains sulforaphane, high in vitamin C and K. Supports immune system.

Healthy Eating Tips

Eat the Rainbow

Choose vegetables of different colors to get a variety of nutrients and antioxidants.

Cooking Methods

Steam or roast vegetables to retain their nutritional value and enhance flavor.

Portion Size

Fill half your plate with vegetables for a balanced, healthy meal.
